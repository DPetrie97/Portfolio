About Me

Hey there! My name is David Petrie and I am currently enrolled as a web developer at Eleven Fifty Academy. Since a young age, I’ve been interested in technology, going as far as to mindlessly clicking around the terminal without knowing anything about what I was doing. My professional history begins in customer service and sales where I gained a passion for really getting to know people to assist them best, takes a journey to behavioral therapy where I got more in-depth training and a reminder as to why it is important people look out for each other, and leads me right back to technology and how I can utilize the skills I am learning now to assist others. Having that personal yearning for growth and technology has always kept me eager to learn new skills and brought me back full circle to development.
